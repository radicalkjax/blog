About Me

Hello there! My name is Kali (Kjax || RadicalKjax) and I'm just your friendly neighborhood transfem cyber-junkie. If it's something nerdy, I've probably done it. Lover of video games, anime, physics, computer science and ripping things apart.

I've been known to break things from time to time. As well as building and modding whatever's needed to fit the job. Cars, tractors, industrial equipment, computers and general electronics are some of the things I've poked and prodded.

I run Cybersecurity Sacramento (@cybersac916) / DC916 to help connect the hackers and security enthusiasts of the greater Sacramento area. It's been fun watching the community grow. ❤
