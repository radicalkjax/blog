Kali Jackson

Technical Solutions Specialist | Public-Sector Tech SME
AI SecOps Researcher

Hi there, my name's Kali, I'm just your stereotypical trans girl. I have a distaste for systems that hurt people and enjoy building things that empower marginalized communities.

I'm usually playing games, exploring art or build a giant tech project while poking around the hacking community.

In my profressional life, I'm a Technical Solutions Specialist with 6.5 years of experience as the statewide Subject Matter Expert (SME) and Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) for California's public welfare technology infrastructure. Proven expertise in large-scale device management, procurement, security operations, modernized app development, service migrations, and AI-assisted workflows in the largest human services system in the United States.

I have a strong background in incident response, endpoint security, network troubleshooting, and creating scalable solutions for enterprise environments. Demonstrated ability in engineering, supporting and managing 300+ specialized public-facing devices across all 58 counties in California. Led platform modernization from legacy Java systems to unified React.js architecture while maintaining FedRAMP, StateRAMP, CISA, and NIST compliance for mission-critical government infrastructure.

I specialize in human-centered technology solutions that empower rather than overwhelm users. As former lead and PoC of DEF CON Group DC916, I champion making enterprise security and privacy accessible through education and community outreach.

I'm passionate about sharing my experiences and advocating for inclusivity and diversity. My blog serves as a platform for both technical content and personal reflections on my life's journey.
